TAKE ADVANTAGE OF YOUR BOSCH BENEFITS!

As a Bosch associate, you and your family have access to a comprehensive health and welfare benefits program. Before you enroll in your benefits for the 2016 plan year (April 1, 2016–March 31, 2017), please research your options using this interactive guide. Keep in mind that as a new employee, you have 31 days from the date on your Personalized Enrollment Worksheet (PEW) to enroll in your benefits. If you do not, you will be given default coverage and will not be able to change your coverage until the next Annual Enrollment period, unless you have a qualifying change in family status.

You have a choice of medical plans, dental and vision coverage, and savings and spending accounts. Review them carefully to decide which are right for you.
