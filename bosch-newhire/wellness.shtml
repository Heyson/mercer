Wellness

Bosch wants you and your family to be healthy. That’s why we encourage you to use these wellness resources — and they’re available at no cost to you!

Be Well

As you move forward in your health journey, Bosch is here to help you along. Through Be Well, you have access to free tools and resources to help you maintain and improve your health and well-being, including:

  • Free, annual on-site screenings and flu shots (at larger locations).
  • Free Health Risk Assessments (HRAs).
  • Local wellness activities.
  • Confidential coaching through our wellness partner, Preventure.
  • Weight Watchers (subsidized by Bosch).

The program even offers you financial incentives for completing the biometric screening and HRA, then provides additional services to help you work toward your personal health goals. To learn more about Be Well, visit https://bewell.preventure.com and register with your personnel number.

Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

You and your dependents have access to Bosch’s confidential Employee Assistance Program (EAP), managed by CIGNA Behavioral Health — and it’s available at no cost to you. If you are struggling with a personal concern, crisis, or even an everyday issue, the EAP can be an invaluable resource. You can get assistance with legal questions or concerns, marital or family conflicts, childcare and senior care, financial problems, alcohol/drug issues, stress and emotional issues, and much more. To learn more, log on to www.cignabehavioral.com.

FREE PREVENTIVE CARE

Remember that Bosch medical plans cover in-network preventive care at 100%, with no deductible. This means you pay nothing out-of-pocket for annual physicals, routine blood work, flu shots and vaccinations, screenings, and more.
