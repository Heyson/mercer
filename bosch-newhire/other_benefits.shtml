Other benefits

Bosch provides these additional insurance benefits to help protect your financial security.

LIFE INSURANCE

Bosch automatically provides basic associate life insurance at no cost to you. Your benefit is equal to your annual base pay rounded up to the next $1,000 and multiplied by two. For example, if your pay is $45,300, your annual base coverage is $46,000 times two, or $92,000.

You may reduce the level of your basic associate life insurance to as low as one times your annual base pay, in which case you will receive a credit that can be applied to the cost of your other benefits.

You may also purchase additional associate life insurance coverage up to a maximum of eight times your annual base pay (rounded up to the next $1,000), up to a maximum of $2 million. In addition to choosing this additional, portable life insurance for yourself, you may also elect the following optional coverage for your family:

  • Spouse/domestic partner life insurance (up to age 70): Coverage is available at the following levels: $10,000, $25,000, $50,000, $75,000, or $100,000. Note that your spouse/domestic partner life insurance coverage level can’t exceed the level of your combined basic and optional life insurance coverage.
  • Child life insurance: Coverage is available for your dependent children age six months to age 26 at the following levels: $5,000 and $10,000 per child. For children age one day to six months, the plan pays a maximum of $1,000 per child in the event of death.

Evidence of Insurability (EOI)
If you elect to increase the level of your life insurance benefit for yourself or your spouse/domestic partner, you may need to provide proof of good health, also known as Evidence of Insurability (EOI). Learn more.

Annual base pay
Coverage is based on your pay in effect on the January 1 prior to the beginning of the plan year. Base pay includes your salary, any elective deferred compensation and pre-tax premiums, FSA contributions, and elective 401(k) plan contributions. Base pay does not include any overtime pay, bonuses, shift differential pay, relocation pay, or employer-paid retirement or fringe benefits not normally included in cash compensation.

For sales associates earning commission, any commission sales wages paid in the last 24-month period will be averaged and annualized for this period. The calculated annualized commission amount will then be added to your benefit salary affecting benefit coverage and contributions. This "look back" will be calculated each January 1 with the benefit commission wage used for the entire plan year. For purposes of determining your coverage, your annual base pay will be rounded to the nearest thousand.

Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D) insurance

AD&D insurance provides coverage if you die accidentally or become seriously injured. Bosch automatically provides basic AD&D (associate only) insurance equal to two times your annual base pay rounded up to the next $1,000 and multiplied by two, at no cost to you.

You may reduce the level of your basic AD&D (associate only) coverage to as low as one times your annual base pay, in which case you will receive a credit that can be applied to the cost of your other benefits.

You also have the opportunity to elect additional coverage up to eight times your annual base pay, up to a maximum of $2 million.

Besides choosing additional AD&D coverage for yourself, you may also elect the following forms of optional coverage for your family:

  • Spouse/domestic partner: Benefit would equal 60% of associate AD&D coverage.
  • Child(ren): Benefit would equal 15% of associate AD&D coverage. Coverage is available for your dependent children age six months to age 26.
  • Family: Benefit would equal 50% of associate AD&D coverage for spouse/domestic partner; 10% for children.
Business travel accident insurance

If you die or experience certain covered injuries while traveling on Company business, Bosch will automatically provide you with a business travel accident insurance benefit equal to four times your annual base pay (up to a maximum of $1,000,000). This benefit would be paid in addition to any associate life and AD&D benefits.

Long-term disability (LTD) insurance

LTD protects a portion of your income if illness or injury prevents you from working for more than 180 days. Bosch automatically provides basic LTD coverage to replace 50% of your monthly base pay at no cost to you. In addition to the basic benefit, you have the option to purchase additional LTD coverage for a total income replacement benefit of 65% of your monthly base pay. The combined maximum monthly benefit for basic and optional LTD coverage is $18,000.

Evidence of Insurability
We require EOI if you make a late request for the Long-Term Disability Insurance Buy Up Plan (meaning that you made the request after first becoming eligible for the benefit).

Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

You and your dependents have access to Bosch’s confidential Employee Assistance Program (EAP), managed by CIGNA Behavioral Health — and it’s available at no cost to you. If you are struggling with a personal concern, crisis, or even an everyday issue, the EAP can be an invaluable resource. You can get assistance with legal questions or concerns, marital or family conflicts, childcare and senior care, financial problems, alcohol/drug issues, stress and emotional issues, and much more. To learn more, log on to www.cignabehavioral.com.

Even if you waive elective coverage, you must designate a beneficiary for the Company-paid plans. You can designate a beneficiary online at www.ibenefitcenter.com by clicking on the “Beneficiaries” link in the “myHealth” section of the Web site.
