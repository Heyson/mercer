ENROLL

You must enroll within 31 days of the date on your Personalized Enrollment Worksheet (PEW). Otherwise, you will be given default coverage and will not be able to change your coverage until the next Annual Enrollment period (unless you have a qualifying change in family status). Default coverage includes:

  • Employee-only coverage in the CDHP 80 medical plan option.
  • Basic life insurance coverage equal to two times your annual base pay rounded up to the next $1,000.
  • Basic accidental death & dismemberment (AD&D) coverage equal to two times your base pay.
  • Short-term disability (STD) coverage.
  • Long-term disability (LTD) insurance coverage to replace 50% of your monthly base pay.
  • Business travel accident insurance coverage equal to four times your annual base pay (up to a maximum of $1,000,000).
