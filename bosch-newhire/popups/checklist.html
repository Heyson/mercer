ENROLLMENT CHECKLIST

  • Review your benefit options, using this website as well as the Personalized Enrollment Worksheet (PEW) that you received in the mail.
  • Use the online tools to help you make your benefit decisions. You’ll also find a variety of resources — including a Plan Comparison tool — on the Bosch Benefits Web site at www.ibenefitcenter.com.
  • Follow the easy enrollment steps to make your elections. When enrolling, be sure to enter your life insurance beneficiary information. Please also check to ensure your dependents' Social Security numbers are on file and correct.
  • If you are enrolling in a Consumer Driven Health Plan (CDHP), make sure you activate your Health Savings Account (HSA).
