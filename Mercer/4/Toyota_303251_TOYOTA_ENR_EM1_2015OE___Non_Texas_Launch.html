Begin your 2015 benefits journey.
During Annual Enrollment, November 5 — November 18, 2014, you have the opportunity to enroll in or make changes to your benefits for 2015. These benefits offer important support to help you take care of your and your family's well being.
1. Explore your options. Review the information in the attached Annual Enrollment Guide to learn more about your benefit options, including changes that are being made in 2015. This guide was also mailed to you at home.
2. Get information. Go to mybenefits.toyota.com to compare plans or use the calculators to help you estimate your needs.
3. Make your choices. Enroll in your benefits online at mybenefits.toyota.com or over the phone by calling the Toyota Benefits Center at 1-800-334-4015 between 8:00 a.m. and 10:00 p.m. Eastern Time, Monday through Friday.
