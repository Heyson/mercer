As a current HSA holder, please be aware that effective January 1, 2015, Halliburton has partnered with a new administrator for Health Savings Account services: ConnectYourCare. The purpose of this communication is to advise you of your options regarding your existing Health Savings Account.
If you enrolled in the Consumer Choice PPO (CCP) for 2015, a new Health Savings Account will be opened for you with ConnectYourCare and their custodian partner, HSA bank. You will be able to continue to make convenient deposits via payroll deductions into your Health Savings Account with ConnectYourCare. Halliburton will also providing employer contributions into this account.
Regarding your current Health Savings Account, please be advised that you have the following options available to you:
1. You may continue to keep your existing Health Savings Account with J. P. Morgan Chase. Halliburton will not be funding the account or facilitating direct deposit into the account through payroll deduction. If you choose to keep your Health Savings Account with J. P. Morgan Chase open, please be advised that starting January 1, 2015, you will incur a $2.50 monthly maintenance fee, which will be deducted from your Health Savings Account. You will still be able to access your existing funds for qualified medical expenses.
2. You have the option to roll your existing Health Savings Account funds currently with J. P. Morgan Chase into your new Health Savings Account with ConnectYourCare. If you choose to elect this option, please complete the electronic consent (EConsent) form at Authorization to Transfer. The EConsent must be completed by December 10, 2014. If the EConsent form is not completed by December 10, 2014, your current Health Savings Account will remain at J.P. Morgan Chase.
Please be advised that an HSA to HSA rollover may take between six to eight weeks to fully transfer any funds.
For additional information regarding the new HSA administrator, please refer to your 2015 Annual Enrollment guide, or contact the Halliburton Benefits Center at 866-321-0964. After January 1, 2015, you may contact ConnectYourCare directly at (877) 292-4040 or by visiting www.connectyourcare.com.
