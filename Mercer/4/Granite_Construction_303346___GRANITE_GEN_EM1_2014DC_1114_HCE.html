Dear Granite Retirement Savings Plan Participant,
We would like to share with you important information about spreading your 401(k) contributions throughout the year to maximize Company matching contributions.
Company matching contributions are made on a payroll-by- payroll basis. To obtain the maximum allowable Company match, you need to contribute at least 3% each payroll to receive the full Company match. Meeting the IRS limit ($18,000 for the 2015 plan year and $6,000 for catch-up contributions) too early in the year may prevent you from getting the most Company match available. Conversely, waiting until later in the year to contribute at least 3% means that you may be missing out on the Company match.
For a helpful calculator on how to spread your deferrals over the entire year, log in to www.ibenefitcenter.com.
Click on the “Resource Center” tab.
Go to the “Tools” tab.
Under the “Investing Basics” section, select “How can Employees Maximize Their Employer’s 401(k) Match?”
Log in to the Plan’s website, www.ibenefitcenter.com, or call 1-800-685-6542 today to change your deferral percentage.
