Eligibility

You can open a Health Savings Account (HSA) if you meet all of the criteria set by the Medicare Act of 2003 (which established HSAs). To participate, you:

  • Must be enrolled in a qualified high deductible health plan like Hillenbrand's Consumer Choice Plans.
  • Cannot simultaneously participate in a traditional Health Care Flexible Spending Account (FSA).
  • Cannot be covered by health insurance other than a high deductible health plan like Hillenbrand's Consumer Choice Plans (for example, through your spouse’s employer’s plan).
  • Cannot be enrolled in Medicare or Medicaid.
  • Cannot be claimed as a dependent on someone else's tax return.

To enroll, log on to hillenbrandbenefits.com
