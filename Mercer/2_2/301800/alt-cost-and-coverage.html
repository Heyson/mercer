Cost and coverage details

When it’s time to pay for your health care services, follow these guidelines.

Preventive care – Covered at 100%, so you pay nothing.

Non-preventive care – Follow a simple 3-step process to pay for your non-preventive medical expenses and prescription drugs:

  1. You pay 100% of your initial medical costs until you meet your annual deductible.
  2. After you meet your annual deductible, the plan pays a certain percentage (known as “coinsurance”) of any further costs until you reach your annual out-of-pocket maximum.  
  3. Once you meet your out-of-pocket maximum, the plan pays 100% of your eligible expenses for the remainder of the year.

You'll only follow this process as far as needed in any given year â€“ for example, if you don't meet your annual deductible, steps two and three won't apply.
