Dental plans

You may choose between two dental plans: the 80% Preventive Plan (without orthodontia) or the 100% Preventive Plan (with orthodontia). Although each plan allows you to visit any dentist you choose, you will save more when you go to one of the in-network providers MetLife has contracted with to provide services to our participants at reduced rates.

USE THE TOOLS

Dental plan participants can use MetLife’s website at www.metlife.com/mybenefits to:

  • Find an in-network dentist.
  • Find out what your plan covers.
  • Learn about dental health and more.
FIND A PROVIDER

To locate a MetLife provider, log on to www.metlife.com/mybenefits or call MetLife’s Customer Service Department at 800-942-0854. Note that the dental network is PDPPlus. If you are searching for an in-network provider, you need to choose this option from the drop-down menu.
