A HEALTHY PARTNERSHIP

Marsh & McLennan Companies is committed to partnering with you to have a positive impact on your health, finances, and career. We are proud to support this partnership by offering you a comprehensive and competitive benefits program.

Your role in our partnership starts with making informed choices during Annual Enrollment, but continues all year long as you make decisions about your health and health care.

This Annual Enrollment, give some thought to these questions before you select your coverage. Doing so can also help you manage your care wisely throughout the year.

  • How much health care — and what type of care — did you need in 2013?
  • Will your needs be similar in 2014? Do you foresee changes?
  • How important is cost to you when choosing a plan?
  • How much freedom and flexibility do you need or want when it comes to choosing physicians and hospitals?
  • How much do you think your out-of-pocket health care or dependent care costs will be in 2014?
  • How much life insurance and disability insurance do you need to feel comfortable and well protected?
  • Do you have any other coverage available to you or your family members (for example, through your spouse’s or domestic partner’s employer)?
