ELIGIBILITY SCENARIOS

1. Sarah is an MMC colleague who was hired before June 3, 2013. Sarah has been married for five years to Paul who works at another company. Sarah and Paul are enrolled in Paul’s company health insurance. Paul loses his job in 2014 and Sarah enrolls the couple in MMC medical coverage. Are Sarah and Paul eligible for the HealthyMe rate?

a. If Sarah and Paul completed the three Know Your Numbers steps by September 13, 2013 to qualify for the HealthyMe rate in 2014, they are eligible for the HealthyMe rate immediately when Sarah enrolls them in MMC medical coverage in 2014.

b. If Sarah and Paul did not complete the three Know Your Numbers steps by September 13, 2013 to qualify for the HealthyMe rate in 2014, they are eligible for the Standard rate when Sarah enrolls them in MMC medical coverage in 2014.

c. If either Sarah or Paul (but not both) completed the three Know Your Numbers steps by September 13, 2013, they are eligible for the Blended rate when Sarah enrolls them in MMC medical coverage in 2014.

2. Matt is a Marsh & McLennan Companies colleague who was hired before June 3, 2013. Matt is engaged to Jen. Matt and Jen get married in November 2013 and he enrolls Jen in MMC medical coverage. Is Jen eligible for the HealthyMe rate in 2014?

a. If Matt and Jen did not satisfy the plan’s criteria for domestic partnership (as described in the Benefits Handbook) before June 3, 2013, Jen is eligible for the HealthyMe rate when Matt enrolls her in MMC medical coverage in 2014.

b. If Matt and Jen had satisfied the plans’ criteria for domestic partnership (as described in the Benefits Handbook) before June 3, 2013 and Jen did not complete the three Know Your Numbers steps by September 13, 2013 to qualify for the HealthyMe rate in 2014, Jen is not eligible for the HealthyMe rate.

3. Hal is an MMC colleague who was hired before June 3, 2013. Hal has been married for ten years to Nancy who works at another company. Hal is enrolled in MMC medical coverage and Nancy is covered under her company medical coverage. Hal completes the Know Your Numbers steps by September 13, 2013 and is eligible for the HealthyMe rate in 2014. Nancy does not complete the Know Your Numbers steps by September 13, 2013. Nancy loses her job in 2014 and Hal enrolls Nancy in MMC medical coverage. Does Nancy qualify for the HealthyMe rate?

No, since Nancy and Hal were married before June 3, 2013 and Nancy did not complete the three Know Your Numbers steps by September 13, 2013, she is not eligible for the HealthyMe rate. Since Hal completed the three Know Your Numbers steps by September 13, 2013 and is eligible for the HealthyMe rate in 2014, Hal and Nancy qualify for the Blended rate in 2014.
