Enrolling is easy

What is your enrollment deadline? What happens if you do not enroll?

ENROLL ONLINE
  • Visit the Bosch Benefits Web site at www.ibenefitcenter.com. Register your account by clicking "Get Started" and following the prompts. As part of the account setup process, you will be asked to create a user name and password. You will also be asked to give answers to several security questions, which will allow you to retrieve your login information if you forget it in the future.
  • Click "Complete Your New Hire Enrollment Elections" and follow the prompts to enroll in your benefits.
NEED ASSISTANCE?
Call the Bosch Benefits Center at 800-207-9012 (857-362-5996 internationally) between 9:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m. Eastern time, any business day.
